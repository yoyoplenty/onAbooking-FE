Description

Welcome to OnA Apartment, where luxury meets comfort in the heart of Lekki, Lagos, Nigeria! Indulge in the ultimate getaway experience with our exquisite 4-bedroom duplex luxury apartments featuring stunning views and top-notch amenities. Unwind by our serene outdoor pool or explore the vibrant cityscape just moments away. Your perfect retreat awaits. Book your stay now and elevate your Lekki experience with OnA Apartment.

Our Amenities

With free Wifi, this 4-bedroom duplex apartment provides a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and oven. The unit has soundproofing and a bath. The apartment offers bed linen, towels, and laundry service.
